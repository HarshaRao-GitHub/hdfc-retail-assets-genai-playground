HDFC LIMITED — PRODUCT CIRCULAR (SYNTHETIC)
============================================
Circular No: RA/PROD/2025/001
Date: January 5, 2025
Subject: Special Home Loan Scheme — Republic Day Offer
Effective: January 15, 2025 to March 31, 2025

TO: All Branch Heads, Regional Managers, Relationship Managers — Retail Assets

Dear Colleagues,

We are pleased to announce the Republic Day Special Home Loan Scheme to drive Q4 disbursements:

1. RATE CONCESSION
   - Salaried (CIBIL 750+): Additional 0.10% concession over prevailing rate
   - Women borrowers: Additional 0.05% concession (stackable with above)
   - Combined maximum concession: 0.15% off standard rate
   - Effective rate for best profile: 8.25% p.a.

2. PROCESSING FEE WAIVER
   - 100% processing fee waiver for loans sanctioned during scheme period
   - Applicable to Home Loan and Balance Transfer products only
   - LAP: 50% processing fee concession

3. TOP-UP INCENTIVE
   - Existing customers availing top-up: NIL processing fee + 0.10% rate concession
   - Top-up approval within 48 hours for customers with 24+ clean EMIs

4. BALANCE TRANSFER SPECIAL
   - BT rate: Starting 8.35% (best in market)
   - Top-up on BT: Available at BT rate (no separate pricing)
   - Target: 500 BT cases during scheme period

5. BUILDER TIE-UP INCENTIVE
   - Empaneled builders offering possession in 2025: Fast-track processing (7-day sanction guarantee)
   - Shared marketing fund: Up to INR 2L per builder for co-branded campaigns

6. SCHEME TARGETS & INCENTIVES FOR RMs
   | Criteria                        | Target | Incentive           |
   |---------------------------------|--------|---------------------|
   | Disbursements during scheme     | +20%   | 0.03% of incremental|
   | BT cases                        | 5+     | INR 2,500 per case  |
   | Cross-sell (CASA + Insurance)   | 50%    | INR 1,000 per case  |
   | Digital sanction (app-based)    | 30%    | INR 500 per case    |

7. ELIGIBILITY
   - All home loan products (HL-SAL, HL-SE, HL-NRI, HL-BT, HL-TU)
   - Not applicable to LAP-Commercial
   - Minimum loan amount: INR 10 lakhs
   - Customer must complete KYC and submit all docs by March 15 for March 31 disbursement

8. MARKETING SUPPORT
   - Digital ads budget allocated: INR 50L across Google/Meta
   - Branch standees and collateral dispatched by Jan 12
   - Customer SMS campaign: 2 waves (Jan 15 and Feb 15)
   - Microsite: hdfc.com/republic-day-home-loan (live Jan 14)

ACTION REQUIRED:
1. All Branch Heads to conduct team briefing by Jan 12
2. RMs to identify top 20 pipeline cases for scheme conversion
3. Weekly MIS reporting starts Jan 20
