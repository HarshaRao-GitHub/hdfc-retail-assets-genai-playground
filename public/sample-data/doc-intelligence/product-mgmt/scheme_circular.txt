   STEP 6: TRACKING & REPORTING (Weekly)

10. OBJECTION HANDLING GUIDE

   The following are the 8 most common customer objections RMs encounter during scheme campaigns,
   along with recommended responses. Practice these during your daily team huddle.

   OBJECTION 1: "SBI/LIC HFL is offering a lower rate than HDFC."
   RESPONSE: "I understand rate comparison is important. Let me show you the full picture:
   Our effective rate with the Republic Day concession can go as low as 8.25%, which is very
   competitive. But beyond the rate, consider our 48-hour in-principle approval, zero processing
   fee during this scheme, and our digital journey that saves you 3-4 branch visits. When you add
   up the convenience and speed, the total cost of borrowing from HDFC is often lower. Let me
   calculate your exact EMI with our offer so we can compare apples to apples."

   OBJECTION 2: "I want to wait for rates to come down further."
   RESPONSE: "That's a valid thought. However, consider this: property prices in your preferred area
   have been rising 5-8% annually. Even if rates drop by 25 bps (which is uncertain), a 3-month
   delay could cost you INR 2-4 lakhs more on the property price. Also, this scheme's zero
   processing fee and rate concession are time-bound — they expire March 31. You can always
   refinance later if rates drop significantly, but locking in the property at today's price is
   the bigger financial decision. Shall I show you the math?"

   OBJECTION 3: "The processing fee is too high." / "Can you waive the processing fee?"
   RESPONSE: "Great news — under our Republic Day scheme, the processing fee is already 100%
   waived for home loans and balance transfers. For LAP, we're offering a 50% concession. This
   is a limited-period benefit that saves you INR 25,000-50,000 on a typical loan. This alone
   makes it worth applying during this window."

   OBJECTION 4: "I'm not sure I'll get approved. My CIBIL score is low."
   RESPONSE: "Let's not assume — many customers are pleasantly surprised. Can I do a quick
   pre-check for you? It takes just 5 minutes and doesn't affect your score. We have flexible
   assessment methods, especially for self-employed customers. If your score is above 650, we
   have pathways to approval. And if it's borderline, I can guide you on steps to improve it
   within 30-60 days so you can still apply within the scheme period."

   OBJECTION 5: "I've already applied at another bank. It's too late to switch."
   RESPONSE: "It's not too late at all. If you haven't signed the final loan agreement, you can
   still switch. Many customers come to us mid-process because of our faster turnaround. We can
   match or beat whatever offer you have and still complete processing within the scheme period.
   In fact, our BT rate starting at 8.35% is the best in the market. Let me do a quick comparison
   for you — no obligation."

   OBJECTION 6: "I need to discuss with my family / spouse before deciding."
   RESPONSE: "Absolutely — this is a family decision and should be taken together. Can I help
   by preparing a detailed EMI calculation sheet that you can share with your family? I can also
   set up a call or meeting where both you and your spouse can ask questions together. The key
   thing is to lock in the scheme benefits before March 31 — the application itself is free and
   you can always decide later at the sanction stage."

   OBJECTION 7: "The property I want is not from an HDFC-approved builder."
   RESPONSE: "HDFC approves properties, not just builders. Even if the builder isn't on our
   empaneled list, we can evaluate the specific property. We approve independent properties,
   resale flats, and plots as well. Let me take the property details and check eligibility —
   it usually takes just 2-3 working days. Many customers assume non-empaneled means rejected,
   but that's not the case."

   OBJECTION 8: "I can arrange funds from family. I don't need a loan."
   RESPONSE: "That's great that you have family support. However, consider the tax benefits of
   a home loan: Section 24 allows up to INR 2L deduction on interest, and Section 80C allows
   up to INR 1.5L on principal repayment. For someone in the 30% tax bracket, that's INR 1.05L
   annual tax saving. At our current rates with the scheme concession, the effective cost of
   borrowing after tax benefits is only about 5.5-6.0%. Your family funds could earn more in
   mutual funds or FDs. Would you like me to show you a comparison?"

11. SUCCESS STORIES FROM PREVIOUS CAMPAIGNS

   The following are synthetic case studies from previous festive campaigns to illustrate
   effective RM strategies. Use these as inspiration and share during team huddles.

   CASE STUDY 1: "THE DIWALI BLITZ" — Andheri West Branch (Diwali Campaign 2024)
   ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   RM: Priya Mehta (3 years experience)
   Challenge: Branch was at 72% of quarterly target with 6 weeks remaining.
   Strategy:
   - Priya identified 45 WARM leads from the CRM who had enquired in Jul-Sep but didn't convert
   - She personally called each one, leading with the Diwali rate concession and zero processing fee
   - For 12 leads who cited "waiting for better rates", she used the property appreciation argument
   - She organized 3 builder site camps at major Andheri projects, generating 28 fresh walk-ins
   - She cross-sold HDFC CASA accounts to 18 of her home loan customers (INR 1,000 incentive each)
   Results:
   - 22 sanctions and 17 disbursements during the 6-week campaign (vs. 8 in previous 6 weeks)
   - Branch achieved 108% of quarterly target
   - Priya earned INR 1,42,000 in incremental incentives
   - Recognized as "RM of the Quarter" at regional review
   Key Lesson: Structured re-engagement of WARM leads is often more productive than chasing new leads.

   CASE STUDY 2: "THE BT SPECIALIST" — Gurgaon Sector 56 Branch (Independence Day Campaign 2024)
   ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   RM: Rohit Kapoor (5 years experience)
   Challenge: BT target of 15 cases seemed ambitious for a branch with historically 3-4 BT cases/quarter.
   Strategy:
   - Rohit obtained bureau data on HDFC's catchment area customers with home loans at higher rates
   - Focused on customers paying >9.5% at NBFCs and HFCs (biggest rate savings opportunity)
   - Created a simple one-page "savings calculator" comparing current EMI vs HDFC BT rate
   - Personally visited 8 major residential societies in Sector 56, 54, and 48 during evenings
   - Set up a "Home Loan Health Check" camp at the Sector 56 RWA community center (Sunday)
   - Partnered with 2 CA firms to identify their clients with high-rate loans
   Results:
   - 23 BT cases sanctioned (153% of target) — highest in the region
   - Average ticket size: INR 38 lakhs; Average rate savings for customer: 1.8%
   - 14 out of 23 BT customers also took a top-up (additional INR 5.2 Cr disbursement)
   - Rohit earned INR 57,500 in BT incentives + INR 18,000 in cross-sell incentives
   Key Lesson: Proactive outreach to residential societies and CA partnerships can unlock a large BT pipeline.

   CASE STUDY 3: "THE DIGITAL FIRST RM" — Whitefield Branch, Bangalore (Navratri Campaign 2024)
   ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   RM: Sneha Iyer (2 years experience)
   Challenge: Tech-savvy customer base in Whitefield expected a fully digital experience.
   Strategy:
   - Sneha used the WhatsApp Business integration to send personalized offer messages to 200 leads
   - She created short 60-second video explainers (using approved templates) on the scheme benefits
   - Used the HDFC App RM mode to initiate 80% of applications digitally from customer's location
   - Set up a virtual "Ask HDFC" session on Zoom every Saturday morning (15-min group briefing)
   - For NRI customers in the Whitefield corridor, she offered weekend video calls for documentation
   Results:
   - 35 applications in 4 weeks (vs. branch average of 18)
   - 92% of applications were fully digital — no branch visit needed
   - 28 sanctions, 24 disbursements; disbursement value: INR 9.6 Cr
   - Customer NPS for her cases: 87 (vs. branch average 72)
   - Sneha's digital adoption rate was showcased at the national sales conference
   Key Lesson: Younger, tech-forward RMs should lean into digital tools — customers in IT corridors respond
   strongly to WhatsApp, video calls, and app-based applications over traditional branch visits.
